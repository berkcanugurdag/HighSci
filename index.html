Why HighSci?

HighSci is a yearly event that aims to simulate a college level conference experience and prepare youth for college by helping the participants acquire paper writing and research skills.

!

Currently, due to the global pandemic of Coronavirus, the conference is not yet planned to be face to face. It will preferably be via Zoom or other remote meeting softwares, but according to how the realization of this event goes, there is a chance that other methods can be utilized.
